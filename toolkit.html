Clinical Topics Addressed by the HIV PROs

“What do we want to know about our patients that would allow us to do a better job—to increase our knowledge and take action?”
- Clinic Director, speaking about their implementation experience

The HIV PROs system assesses the patient's experience of clinically important issues, using standardized questionnaires. This information is organized by the following clinical domains, inspected for critical values or trends, and displayed to depict changes over time. Alerts for critical issues such significant anxiety or distress, interference with function, or suicidal ideation, may be sent to an appropriate provider or displayed to the patient, depending on whether the patient is within or outside a clinical setting when using the PROs.
