Implementation Toolkit

PRO implementation involves several stages of planning and execution, as well as follow-up and assessment. The toolkit linked below has been designed to support PRO uptake in routine HIV care by highlighting key considerations for implementers; this includes implementation insights, example materials and testimonies from stakeholders who have first-hand experience with PRO implementation, covering key areas that need to be actioned or assessed. The objective is to facilitate easier and more effective PRO use where appropriate.

Implementation Steps

  1. Choosing the right PROs and implementation approaches to meet patient and clinic needs
  2. Identifying and obtaining stakeholder Buy-in
  3. Making the business case to secure funding or endorsement for PRO Implementation
  4. Setting up the PROs to be administered electronically and considering potential approaches connecting the electronic PRO (ePRO) system to electronic health records
  5. Integrating PROs into the clinic flow including considerations of when and where to administer them
  6. Training clinic personnel on what they need to know about the PRO implementation and how they may engage with patients
  7. Monitoring, evaluation and updating the PRO system used in a clinic
